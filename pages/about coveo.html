About Coveo

We are in the experience economy. Being digital is only table stakes. People expect experiences that are relevant and effortless. The Coveo Relevance Cloud™ leverages the power of search, data, and AI so that every business can serve people in very individual ways, and so that every business can participate in the experience economy on their own terms.

Relevance wins Innovation is in our blood.

From a single search to an entire user journey, increase the relevance of your experiences at every touchpoint. Easily index content and crunch loads of data. So you can bring your customers, partners, or teams to the right content sooner. Get 360° relevance for Ecommerce, Service, and the Workplace.

Accurately predict what each user will want and automatically display the most relevant content for them. Connect search insights to interaction data from any touchpoint for a truly seamless experience.

We are passionately committed to the principle that relevant digital experiences are a requirement for winning in a competitive, sophisticated marketplace powered by AI and data. Relevance transforms the outcome of any digital interaction, starting with search, and extending to prescriptive experiences that drive shopping conversion rates and cart size, enhance self-service and service intelligence, and make employees more proficient. Today, relevance is within reach for all businesses, not just the tech-first giants. Coveo has democratized AI and made it possible for every business to achieve greatness on their own terms.
